About

The Art of Rotoscoping

Rotoscoping is the process of tracing over live-action footage, frame by frame, to produce realistic animation. I sourced a YouTube video and methodically traced each frame — layering hand-drawn lines over real movement to create a fluid, stylized animation.

This project reflects my passion for animation and my growing skills in multimedia production. Through this process, I explored how technology and creativity work together to produce engaging visual content.
